Site statements

Accessibility, privacy and copyright

These statements are standard text for the static site and should be reviewed before publication.

Accessibility

This site aims to meet WCAG 2.2 AA standards. It uses semantic HTML, keyboard-accessible navigation, readable text sizes, strong colour contrast and descriptive link text. If you need a resource in another format, please use the contact form on this page and we will help.

Privacy

When you submit the contact form, the name, email address and message you provide are sent to the project team by email and used only to respond to your enquiry. The form uses Cloudflare Turnstile to prevent spam. We do not sell your details or add you to any mailing list without your consent.

Copyright

Unless otherwise stated, site content is © The Grenfell Curriculum. Resources may be used for educational purposes with attribution, subject to the licence or permissions stated on each individual resource.
