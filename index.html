The Grenfell Curriculum

Supporting thoughtful, respectful and justice-centred teaching about Grenfell.

The project brings together community-engaged research, teacher professional development, classroom resources, policy work and public education.

Access the teacher CPD Browse resources

A project about education, memory and justice

The Grenfell Tower fire took place on 14 June 2017 in North Kensington, London. The Grenfell Curriculum starts from the understanding that Grenfell is not only a past tragedy, but an ongoing matter of memory, justice, accountability, housing, regulation, inequality, community and education.
