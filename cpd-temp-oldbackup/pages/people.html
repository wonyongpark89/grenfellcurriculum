The Grenfell Education Working Group

This advisory group consists of survivors and bereaved families from the Grenfell Tower fire, alongside educators in Kensington and Chelsea. In 2025, the research team and the Working Group collaborated to design the CPD, ensuring the curriculum remains rooted in community voice and trauma-informed practice.
