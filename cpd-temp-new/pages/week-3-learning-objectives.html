Week 3: Learning Objectives

By the end of Week 3, you will be able to:

1

Analyse responses to the Grenfell Tower fire and evaluate their impacts on the local community and wider society.

2

Explain recent developments related to Grenfell, with particular attention to memorialisation and justice processes.

3

Apply an understanding of the temporal dimensions of Grenfell (before, during, and after the fire) to inform instructional decisions.

4

Design a learning experience about Grenfell that is grounded in Education for Disaster Justice (EDJ) principles.
