What Should Learners Be Able to Do?

In this video, we move beyond what learners should know about Grenfell to explore what they should be able to do, outlining six key competencies drawn from community research that translate justice-centred education into meaningful action.

It highlights the importance of critically analysing decision-making, applying professional ethics, practising empathy and active listening, engaging in community action, and developing critical media literacy to help build a more accountable and compassionate society.
