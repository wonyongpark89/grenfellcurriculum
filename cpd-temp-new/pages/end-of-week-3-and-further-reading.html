CPD Week 3

End of Week 3 & Further Reading

As you complete this programme, we invite you to carry forward the principles at its heart: listening carefully, centring community voices, and approaching Grenfell with both compassion and critical awareness. Teaching about the fire is not only about recounting events, but about honouring lives, recognising injustice, and supporting young people to think ethically about the world around them.

Thank you for your commitment to this work. By engaging thoughtfully with this course, you contribute to a living legacy, one that upholds dignity, truth, and justice in education, and ensures that the lessons of Grenfell continue to be heard, understood, and acted upon.

 

Further Reading

The Inquiry & Accountability

1 Phase 2 Report: Digest of Key Findings, Fire Brigades Union
Accessible summary of the 1,700-page final report’s most important conclusions.
2 The Grenfell Tower Inquiry, Royal Borough of Kensington and Chelsea
The council’s response, including adoption of the Hillsborough Charter.
3 Grenfell Tower Inquiry: House of Lords Debate, Lords Library
Parliamentary briefing covering the Inquiry, criminal investigations, and remediation.
4 Grenfell Testimony Week
An independent initiative giving the bereaved, survivors, and residents a platform to speak directly to the organisations responsible for the fire.
5 How the Failures That Caused Grenfell Still Exist Today, The Guardian (February 2026)
Video report on systemic failures that remain unresolved years after the fire.

Justice Campaigns & Community Response

6 Grenfell United
Survivors’ and bereaved families’ campaign for justice, safety reform, and criminal charges.
7 Justice4Grenfell
Community-led campaign documenting the fight for accountability since 2017.
8 Shouts for Justice at Grenfell Anniversary March, ITV News (June 2025)
Coverage of the eighth anniversary silent walk, likely the last before demolition.

Memorialisation & the Tower’s Future

9 Remembering Grenfell: Recommendations and Next Steps, Memorial Commission (PDF, 2023)
The community-led Commission’s recommendations for a “sacred space” memorial.
10 Update on the Future of Grenfell Tower, GOV.UK (February 2025)
The government’s decision to dismantle the tower and plans for a memorial.
11 Two-Year Process to Take Down Grenfell Tower Begins, ITV News (Sept 2025)
Reporting on the start of floor-by-floor deconstruction of the tower.
12 Grenfell Tower to Be Demolished, Sparking Outrage, The Architect’s Newspaper
The contested demolition decision and the five shortlisted memorial design teams.
