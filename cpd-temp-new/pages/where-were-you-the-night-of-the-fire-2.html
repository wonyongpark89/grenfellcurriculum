CPD Week 2

Where Were You The Night Of The Fire?

Before looking closely at the Inquiry timeline, pause to locate your own memory of the night of 14 June 2017. This reflection is not about testing recall. It is about noticing how disasters enter public consciousness differently depending on where we were, what media reached us, and how close we felt to the community affected.

Reflect

Can you remember what you were doing that night? When was the first time you heard about the fire? What shaped your first understanding of what had happened?
