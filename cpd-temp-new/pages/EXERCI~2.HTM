Exercise: Design Your Grenfell Learning Experience

Now that you have explored the historical context of the Grenfell Tower fire, the justice-centred framework for learning, and the CARE–LEAD model for responsible pedagogy, it is time to begin applying this knowledge in practice.

Based on what you have learned about the Grenfell Tower fire and effective approaches to teaching sensitive and complex topics, begin designing a lesson plan. Consider your educational purpose, the age and context of your learners, the resources you will use, and how you will ensure the lesson is ethical, trauma-informed, and accountable to the community’s experiences.
