Further Reading

Community & Everyday Life

1 The Grenfell Tower Fire — London Museum
Museum exhibition page on the tower, its residents, and London’s housing inequalities.
2 In Brutal Presence — Nicola Muirhead (LensCulture)
Documentary photography and resident testimony from North Kensington before and after the fire.
3 Grenfell: Before the Fire — London Intelligence
Profiles of residents and a portrait of the community that lived in the tower.

Warnings & Resident Activism

4 Grenfell Action Group Blog
The residents’ blog that documented fire safety warnings from 2012 onwards.
5 “KCTMO — Playing with Fire!” (November 2016 blog post, PDF)
The blog post that predicted a catastrophic fire — eight months before it happened.
6 A Residents’ Group Made Repeated Warnings — BuzzFeed News
Overview of the Grenfell Action Group’s eight fire safety warnings over three years.

Structural Injustice & Policy

7 Six Key Failures in the Way Grenfell Was Managed — Inside Housing
Specialist housing journalism on management failures before the fire.
8 Following Grenfell — Equality and Human Rights Commission
EHRC inquiry into human rights and equality failings before and after the fire.
9 Grenfell Warnings and the Collapse of Local Journalism — The Conversation
How the decline of local news meant residents’ warnings went unreported.
