Further Reading

What Happened That Night

1 Grenfell Tower Inquiry Phase 1 Report (October 2019)
Official findings on the events of the night: fire spread, evacuation, and emergency response.
2 Grenfell Tower Fire — London Fire Brigade
LFB’s account of the night and the institutional changes that followed.
3 The Grenfell Tower Fire — Understanding Regulation
Clear summary of the technical and regulatory failures that enabled the fire to spread.

Survivors’ Voices & Testimony

4 Grenfell United
The survivors’ and bereaved families’ campaign group — their story in their words.
5 Grenfell Testimony Week
A project dedicated to giving the bereaved, survivors, and residents a platform to share their experiences.
6 “GRENFELL TOWER FIRE” — Grenfell Action Group (14 June 2017)
The community blog’s raw post on the day of the fire: “All our warnings fell on deaf ears.”

Media Failure

7 Finally, the Truth About Hillsborough — The Conversation
Analysis of media failure at Hillsborough — a key comparator for teaching about Grenfell.
