Remembering Grenfell

As the inquiry has progressed and criminal investigations continue, the focus has increasingly turned to a deeper question: How should we remember Grenfell? What is the legacy of the fire, and what have we truly learnt?

Watch: Grenfell Tower Memorial Commission — Eighth Anniversary Film
The Grenfell Tower Memorial Commission

Following the tragic events, the Grenfell Tower Memorial Commission was established to develop a truly community-led vision for a fitting memorial — one that commemorates those who lost their lives and those whose lives were forever changed. The Commission began its work in September 2019.

They worked closely with the Grenfell community — bereaved families, survivors, Lancaster West Estate residents, and the wider neighbourhood who lived in close proximity to the tower — to understand their hopes and wishes for a future memorial. The result was a set of recommendations published in their 2023 report, Remembering Grenfell.
