CPD Week 1

Week 1: Learning Objectives

← PeopleAbout You →

By the end of Week 1, you will be able to:

1

Describe everyday life in Grenfell Tower and the surrounding community prior to the 2017 fire.

2

Explain the structural, social, and political injustices that created the conditions in which the fire became a disaster.

3

Understand Education for Disaster Justice (EDJ) and apply it to the analysis of specific disasters.
