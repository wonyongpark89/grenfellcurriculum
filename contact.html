Site statements

Accessibility, privacy and copyright

These statements are standard text for the static site and should be reviewed before publication.

Accessibility

This site aims to meet WCAG 2.2 AA standards. It uses semantic HTML, keyboard-accessible navigation, readable text sizes, strong colour contrast and descriptive link text. If you need a resource in another format, please contact the project team using the form on this page.

Privacy

This site does not collect personal data except where information is submitted through the contact form. Form submissions are used only to respond to enquiries. Any future analytics, mailing lists or additional forms should be covered by an updated privacy statement.

Copyright

Unless otherwise stated, site content is © The Grenfell Curriculum. Resources may be used for educational purposes with attribution, subject to the licence or permissions stated on each individual resource.
